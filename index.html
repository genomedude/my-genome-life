Understanding Genetic Blueprints

Think of your DNA as a blueprint for building a house. The blueprint outlines the structural frame, but it does not select the final building components. It is entirely up to you whether you build with low-grade materials or sturdy bricks, mortar, and premium tile. Your raw data is just the paperwork—your vitamins, custom nutrients, diet choices, and environment act as the physical building materials that determine how that house actually stands.

Genes Are Tendencies, Not Certainties

Your raw data tracks genetic vulnerabilities, not your absolute physical destiny. For instance, many individuals carrying the highest-risk Alzheimer's markers maintain pristine cognitive sharpness and never develop the condition. Similarly, some people with MTHFR variations process synthetic folic acid seamlessly with zero issues. Your body possesses biological switches that can be flipped on or off through proper workarounds.
